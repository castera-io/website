About

CAST.ERA is an industry-leading joint venture between Sinclair Broadcasting Group and SK Telecom that combines telecom with media broadcasting technology development.

Dr. Kyungmo Stanley Park

Chief Technology Officer


Stanley has always shown consistent dedication to the digital transformation of NextGen Services, which is visible in his 15 years of industry and research experience in many aspects of media technology and solution for consumer electronics, networks, and distributed environments. Before joining the company, he led SK Telecom's development of 5G-ATSC3.0, a convergence technology innovated with Broadcasting and Mobile/OTT streaming. At Samsung Electronics Co., Ltd, Stanley worked with the multimedia system technology & research laboratory— and led the development of 5G and ATSC 3.0 systems at the company's advanced research & development hub known as Samsung Research. Also, Stanley made his mark in various organizations; he was a Chairman of the cloud-media & media transport workgroup in MPEG and a member of 3GPP and ATSC for the International Standard activity.


Stanley's achievements are recorded in many publications such as papers, patents, and International Standards-related documents on advanced media technology for mobile, multimedia systems, and applications. As a technologist and visionary, he has given numerous talks at major conferences and workshops on business and technology innovations in media system technology and services.

Stanley holds a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from KyungHee University.


Kevin Gage​

Chief Operating Officer - USA

Chief Executive Officer - Republic of Korea


Kevin Gage is a proven leader, enabling industry change and innovation. Along with Dr. Stanley Park, Mr. Gage co-founded CAST.ERA on the idea that leading edge telecom technologies would transform a new era of broadcast services.  Prior to CAST.ERA, Mr. Gage was EVP & CTO of Sinclair’s technology development subsidiary, ONE Media, and led industry efforts to develop a new data delivery capable broadcast standard.  Mr. Gage joined the Broadcast industry when he was appointed NAB’s inaugural EVP, Chief Technology Officer. Prior to joining the Broadcast industry, Mr. Gage led the development of transformative industry changing digital platforms such as iTunes, DVD and Hulu while working at Time Warner and NBC.
