Cloud Platform Station In A Box

  • Broadcast using telecom industry-proven cloud platform
  • 24/7 operation with resource optimization and high availability

Vehicle TV & eGPS

Based on the ATSC3.0 mobile capability, Vehicle TV receives the multi-channel broadcasting service from CAST.ERA’s Cloud-based ATSC3.0 System. It supports the targeted advertising that meets the individual passenger's preference.

SE Device for Vehicle TV:

1. ATSC3.0 mobile reception (U.S, Korean)

2. Targeted Ad Insertion – NRT Datacasting or OTT

3. OTA – OTT synchronization

4. FOTA – AVN (3D map update, firmware update)

Better Quality

  • Resolution: 720p to 1080p, 1080p to 2160p
  • Color-gamut: SDR (BT.709) to HDR (BT.2020)
  • Frame-rate: 30fps to 120fps, 60fps to 240fps
