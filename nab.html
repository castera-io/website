Edge Gateway Appliance (EGA)

Based on the ATSC3.0 mobile capability, Vehicle TV receives the multi-channel broadcasting service from CAST.ERA’s Cloud based ATSC3.0 System. In addition, it supports the targeted advertising that meets the individual passenger's preference.

Using artificial intelligence(AI) algorithms that continuously optimize video quality, AI-Upscaler can enhance the video resolution, and increase frame-rate and color space of HDR to provide a deeper, richer video experience.

Telcom industry-proven distributed cloud-based ATSC3.0 system enables Broadcasters to quickly build the ATSC 3.0 broadcasting system by integrating the existing air-chain solution, composed of physical broadcasting equipment, into a generic server-based virtualized ATSC3.0 Head-End solution with centralized operations management. Edge ATSC 3.0 station is running on the Mobile Edge Computing (MEC) environments, and a Centralized management system (TACO) provides the management of  multiple edge stations.

Edge Gateway Appliance (EGA) is an edge management system that enables multiple broadcasters to securely share a single broadcast spectrum channel. EGA's strength in security can be recognized by allowing the broadcaster (Host) to manage overall spectrum channel sharing and enabling its partner broadcasters (Tenants) to securely manage within the allocated spectrum. The EGA enables a single monitoring solution for ATSC3.0 airchain and datacasting.
